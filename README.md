# Ferocite-Analysis
Power BI dashboard analyzing sales, profit & discount impact
# Business Objective
Analyze sales performance and profitability to understand the impact of discounts, regions, segments, and categories on profit.

## Tools Used
- Power BI
- Power Query
- DAX
- Excel

## Key Analysis Performed
- Sales and profit analysis by category, region, and segment
- Discount vs profitability analysis
- Identification of loss-making discount ranges
- Monthly and regional sales trends
## Key Insights
- Discounts above 20–40% lead to negative profitability
- Technology category performs better under moderate discounts
- Furniture category turns loss-making at higher discounts
- Low discount range (0–10%) delivers highest profit

## Business Recommendation
Implement differentiated discount strategies by category to improve profit margins and reduce losses.
